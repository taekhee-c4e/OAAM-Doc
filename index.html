          • Musical Texture
            • 
                                                    
          • Horizontal – Melodic
            • 
                                                    
          • Vertical – Harmonic
            • 
                                                    
          • Monophonic
                                                        
              
                                                            
            • Single Melodic Line
              • 
                                                        
            
                                                    
            • 
    
                                                    
          • Polyphonic
            • 
                                                    
              
                                                        
            • 2 or more simultaneous melodies
              • 
                                                    
            
                                                    
          • Homophonic
            • 
                                                    
              
                                                        
            • Single Melody with accompaniment
              • 
                                                    
            
                                                    
          • Heterophonic 
            • 
                                                    
              
                                                        
            • The Simultaneous rendering of the slightly difference versions
                                                            of the same melody by two or more performers
